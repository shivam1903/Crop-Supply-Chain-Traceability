PRIVACY POLICY

At Grow Indigo Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to respecting the privacy rights of our customers and all users of our application.

We respect and safeguard the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") which we may receive and collect from you on our application and in the course of our other interactions online.

Information collected:

  • Name (including first name and last name)
  • Email address
  • Company details (if applicable)
  • Phone number
  • Field practice details, field location, crop quantity available for procurement
  • Quantity of produce procured and price per unit
  • Preferred transport
  • IP address, device type, browser type, app version, and usage statistics

Confidentiality and Security

We will keep confidential and protect your Personal Information except where disclosure is required or permitted by law.

We limit access to Personal Information to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

We will take reasonable steps to ensure that any third-party service providers who may have access to the Personal Information are obligated to protect Personal Information on our behalf through confidentiality agreements and otherwise.

The Company adopts reasonable security practices and procedures to protect information from unauthorized access, damage, use, modification, disclosure, or impairment as required by applicable privacy laws.

Accuracy

To the extent that you do provide us with Personal Information, we wish to maintain accurate and current Personal Information. Should you need to update or correct any Personal Information provided by you on the application, please contact us at the following e-mail address info@growindigo.co.in and we will make reasonable efforts to incorporate the changes in your Personal Information that we hold as soon as practicable.
